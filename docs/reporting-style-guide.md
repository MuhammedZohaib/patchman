# Reporting Style Guide

Use this exact finding structure.

## Finding template

- `Title:` precise and short
- `Severity:` Critical | High | Medium | Low | Informational
- `Confidence:` High | Medium | Low
- `CWE / OWASP:` map to the closest standard
- `Affected area:` file, service, endpoint, or workflow
- `Why this matters:` one or two concrete sentences
- `Evidence:` cite code, config, or diff context
- `Exploitability notes:` explain attacker preconditions safely
- `Recommended fix:` practical engineering action
- `Safer example patch:` minimal, non-destructive patch sketch
- `Follow-up checks:` what to verify after remediation

## Tone

- concise
- factual
- evidence-first
- no fear language
- no offensive tutorial content
